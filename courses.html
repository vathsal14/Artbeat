Certification: Earn ABGM-recognized qualifications

What is Mandala Therapy?

Mandalas are geometric designs that symbolize wholeness. Creating or coloring mandalas in art therapy helps calm the mind, restore balance, and reveal hidden emotions through color and pattern.

Free-Form Mandala Creation: Draw from intuition to express feelings

Guided Painting

What is Art Therapy?

Art therapy is a form of psychotherapy that uses creative processes to improve mental, emotional, and physical well-being. Guided by a trained therapist, individuals explore their thoughts and feelings through painting, drawing, sculpting, and other artistic mediums—no prior art experience needed.

How Art Therapy Works

Reduce Stress & Anxiety: Express emotions safely
Process Trauma & Emotions: Heal through art
Improve Self-Awareness: Build confidence and insight
Enhance Problem-Solving: Develop creative coping skills
Develop Coping Mechanisms: Support for all ages

Session Types

One-on-One Therapy: Personalized sessions with a certified therapist
Group Workshops: Supportive space for shared healing
No Artistic Skill Required: Focus on process, not product
Flexible Sessions: In-person or online options
