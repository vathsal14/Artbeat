Our Story

Welcome to Artbeat Studio of Drawing, Painting and Music, a premier creative arts academy founded in 2015. What began as a small studio with a single classroom has blossomed into a thriving creative community that has nurtured thousands of students on their artistic journeys.

Our founder,
D. S. Madhusudan Rao, envisioned a space where creativity knows no bounds and where every individual can discover and develop their artistic voice.

Today, Artbeat stands as a testament to that vision, offering comprehensive programs in visual and performing arts.

"Every student has a unique artistic fingerprint waiting to be discovered."

D. S. Madhusudan Rao

Our Mission

At Artbeat, we believe that artistic expression is not just a skill but a way of life. Our mission is to nurture creativity, inspire self-expression, and provide transformative learning experiences through our specialized Drawing, Painting, and Music programs for students of all ages and skill levels.

Our Approach

We've developed a unique teaching methodology that blends time-tested techniques with contemporary approaches. Our curriculum is carefully structured to progress from fundamental skills to advanced concepts.

What sets us apart is our commitment to personalized attention, with small class sizes and individualized feedback that helps each student progress at their own pace while being part of a supportive creative community.
